About Me

I'm Robin Doak, a passionate software enthusiast from Belfast, Northern Ireland. My journey in software began out of pure curiosity and a love for technology. While I may not have formal education in software development, my dedication to learning and improving has driven me to explore and master various programming languages, frameworks, and tools.

From a young age, I was captivated by the world of technology, spending countless hours understanding how different software works. This hands-on approach allowed me to develop a deep understanding and appreciation for the intricacies of software design and development.

Throughout my self-taught journey, I have worked on numerous projects, ranging from personal endeavors to collaborations with other tech enthusiasts. Each project has been an opportunity to learn, grow, and push the boundaries of my capabilities.

When I'm not coding, I love exploring the beautiful landscapes of Northern Ireland, indulging in photography, and experimenting with new technologies. I'm always excited to take on new challenges and collaborate on innovative projects that can make a positive impact.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. Feel free to explore my work and reach out if you'd like to connect or discuss potential collaborations.
